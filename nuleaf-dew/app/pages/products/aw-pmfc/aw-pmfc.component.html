Unlike other biopower our system doesn’t use combustion and will create enough energy to run itself.

How We Do It

Artificial Wetlands

An artificial wetland (AW) is an engineering ecosystem that treats water with complex biological waste, including black or gray water. It is a park with a purpose that provides natural habitats, fertilizer, produce, and clean water.

Plant Microbial Fuel Cell

A plant microbial fuel cell (PMFC) uses wetland microbes to clean water with simple biological waste. The PMFC further cleans water from the artificial wetland. Energy is created through the microbe’s digestion of the waste.
