What is Biomimicry and Bioinspired Design?

Biomimicry looks to nature for technological inspiration. It emulates nature’s forms, processes, and ecosystems for sustainable designs.

Why use Biomimicry?

For nearly any problem humanity faces, nature has 3.8 billion years of experience in solving it. To date, we have created technology based on human cleverness alone. Life has more elegant solutions at less cost to the planet.

Nature builds safe and reliable materials with eco-friendly processes. Life creates solutions that recycle resources while remaining cost effective and energy efficient. These solutions are self-reliant, interconnected, and built to last

NuLeaf and Bioinspired Design

By looking to nature first, we seek to create wildlife habitats or gardens that are both functional and beautiful. We personalize wastewater treatment while promoting resource and energy independence. By creating biomimetic devices, our products show that sustainability, innovation, and economic growth can be achieved simultaneously.
